    CONCEPT :

    SOIL PROFILE- ENDOMETRIUM PROFILE

    In order to increase the fertility of soil and its productivity a farmer maintains its nutritional balance by treating it with natural fertilizers and herbicides of organic nature. Such preparation is necessary every time every season he wish to successfully grow a crop or produces. In the similar way a mother’s endometrial profile is inescapable factor in order to have a successful pregnancy due to its key role in implantation of embryo and providing nutrition during development. Mother needs intense care and cure before she conceives. In successful pregnancies the endometrium plays an important role in protecting the developing embryo from the maternal immune system. In general concept maternal immune system rejects the embryo because the paternal portion of the embryo’s genetic material makes it partially foreign. But due to the “Maternal Fetal Tolerance” mechanism transitory changes occurs in the maternal immune system which prevents the rejection of the fetus. Most of the pregnancy losses in terms of repeated implantation failure or recurrent miscarriages are due to the inactivity of maternal fetal tolerance mechanism which can be a consequence of unhealthy, unfertile endometrium i.e. impaired endometrial preparedness.
