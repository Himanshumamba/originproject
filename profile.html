OUR PROFILE

DR. PULAKES PURKAIT: FOUNDER Ph.D. (JRF, SRF, PDF)

Dr. Pulakes Purkait is a Molecular Geneticist / Anthropologist. With over a decade of experience in clinical genetics research he has contributed to scientific world at his best. He completed his doctoral research in Clinical Molecular Genetics from Andhra University, Vishakhapatnam. He obtained his post-graduation degree in Biological Anthropology from Vidyasagar University, West Bengal. He has worked on many research projects related to Genetics of Chronic Diseases; Genetics of Breast Cancer, Thalassaemia, Sickle cell Anemia, Genetics of Diabetic Kidney Diseases, DNA polymorphism and Disease genes; Phylogeny of Indian Population-Y chromosome & mtDNA ; Bio-cultural Diversity, and Bio-Cultural Adaptation etc.. He was actively engaged as a Scientist at DOUBLE HELIX, Clinical Cytogenetics and reproductive Immunology Centre, Delhi, India. All his research work has come to the light in form of dozen of research articles published in various journals of repute with international index and many more to come.

1-   Received Junior Research Fellowship (Awarded by Govt. of India) to conduct research at Anthropological survey of India and Indian Council of Medical Research (ICMR). (2008-2012)


2-    Received Senior Research Fellowship (Awarded by Govt. of India) to conduct research at Anthropological survey of India. (2012-2016)


3-    Received Post doctoral Fellowship by University Grant Commission (UGC) action to conduct research at Punjab University, Chandigarh. (2017- )
