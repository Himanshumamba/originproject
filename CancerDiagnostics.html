Cancer Diagnostics services

Cancer is the general name for over 100 medical conditions involving uncontrolled and dangerous cell growth. The disease generally derives from a single cell that is changed dramatically by a series of genetic alterations. A healthy cell has a well defined shape and fits neatly within the ordered array of cells surrounding it.
